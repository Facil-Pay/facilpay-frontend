## Logo Concept

### Design Rationale

- **Symbolism**
  - Abstract payment and network connectivity elements
  - Subtle references to speed and digital flow
- **Visual Style**
  - Modern, clean, and tech-forward
  - Scales clearly from favicon size to large displays
- **Typography**
  - Montserrat wordmark for clarity and readability
  - Balanced spacing for "FacilPay"
